How It Works

Talent gets tested live. Step up and perform 30 seconds of your song set — or go raw with an acapella (no beat). The crowd decides if you Got It or Not It by making noise on the meter.

Pass the test and you hit the stage for your full song. End of the night we crown a champ. Real feedback. Real energy. Real talent.
