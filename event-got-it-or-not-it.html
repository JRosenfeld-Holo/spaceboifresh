How It Works

Step up and perform 30 seconds of your song — or go raw with an acapella, no beat. The crowd decides if you Got It or Not It by applauding and hitting the noise meter.

Pass the test and you earn the stage for your full song. End of the night, we crown a champ. The winner receives the Grand Prize and defends their title the following month.
