SPACEBOIFRESH

Hip-Hop Artist  ·  Radio Host  ·  Audio Engineer
On Air — 97.9 The Beat Dallas

Born in
Oak Cliff.
Built for
The Universe.

Spaceboifresh is a Dallas-based hip-hop artist, record producer, and audio engineer whose sound bridges the gritty soul of Oak Cliff with the boundless ambition of outer space. Raised in the 214, he studied audio engineering at Cedar Valley College, honing the technical precision that now underpins every track he crafts.

As a personality on 97.9 The Beat — Dallas's premier hip-hop and R&B station — Spaceboifresh sits at the intersection of artist and tastemaker. Through his Star Gazing interview series and the viral YO BARS! freestyle platform, he has amplified the voices of rising DFW talent while establishing himself as a pillar of the local scene.

His discography — Dallafornia, For The Low, and Planet EX — charts a sonic journey from sun-soaked West Coast-influenced bounce to deep intergalactic experimentation, all rooted in lived experience.
